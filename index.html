About Me

I'm a game sound designer based in India. I have a passion for creating immersive and engaging audio experiences across game genres. I can score and compose original music, do sound design, record foley, and handle implementation across a range of game engines and middleware, such as Unity, Unreal, FMOD, and Wwise. I have worked on titles remotely, and I'm great at collaborating with distributed teams and shipping projects independently. I've enjoyed the past several years in the game dev industry, building on over decade of experience in audio engineering and live sound.
